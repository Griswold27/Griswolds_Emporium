How to Flip a Pen

Positioning

  1. Hold the pen as if you were writing
  2. Keep your thumb in the last third of the pen
  3. Use your middle finger rather than index and position close to the thumb

The Flick

  1. Push with your middle finger abruptly, but not so much that the pen goes flying
  2. Allow the pen to move around the thumb on its own
  3. Catch the pen with the index finger at the base of your thumb (it should move here naturally)
  4. Practice! It may take time to build muscle memory, but now you can flip a pen
